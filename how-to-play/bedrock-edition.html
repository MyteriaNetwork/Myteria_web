How to Play Myteria

I play on my mobile device.

  • Step 1

    Download the Minecraft app and open it.

    Download Minecraft from your application store and open it.

  • Step 2

    Navigate to the Servers tab.

    Press the "Play" button, then "Servers".

  • Step 3

    Add Myteria as a server.

    Click "Add Server" and set pe.myteria.com as the server address, then click "Save".

  • Step 4

    Finally, join Myteria and play!

    Scroll down, click "Myteria" to join, and start playing!
