How to Play Myteria

I play on my personal computer.

  • Step 1

    Download Minecraft and run it.

    If you have it downloaded but you're unable to play, you may need to download the latest version of Java.

    Download Minecraft
  • Step 2

    Press the Play button to start.

    Our server has cross-version compatibility. The version, Minecraft 1.20.4, is what we recommend.

    Open Minecraft
  • Step 3

    Select Multiplayer and add the server.

    Select Multiplayer and add a new server using our IP address, fun.myteria.com. No need to enter anything else.

    Copy IP address
  • Step 4

    Click Join and we will see you online!

    We can't wait to meet you! If you have questions or simply want to say hello, try joining our Discord!

    Join our Discord
