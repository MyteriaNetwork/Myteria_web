How to Play Myteria

I play on my personal computer.

  • Step 1

    Download Minecraft and run it.

    If you have it downloaded but can't play, you may need the latest version of Java.

    Download Minecraft
  • Step 2

    Press the Play button to start.

    Our server has cross-version compatibility. The version, Minecraft 1.20.4, is recommended.

    Open Minecraft
  • Step 3

    Select Multiplayer and add the server.

    Choose Multiplayer and add a new server using our IP address, fun.myteria.net.

    Copy IP address
  • Step 4

    Click Join and we will see you online!

    Can't wait to meet you! Join our Discord to say hello or ask questions!

    Join our Discord
