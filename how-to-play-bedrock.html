How to Play Myteria

I play on my mobile device.

  • Step 1

    Download the Minecraft app and open it.

    Depending on whether you're on iOS, Android, or Windows 10 Edition, download Minecraft from your application store and open it.

  • Step 2

    Navigate to the Servers tab.

    Once the app is open and you are signed in, press the "Play" button, then "Servers".

  • Step 3

    Add Myteria as a server.

    Click the "Add Server" button. You can name it anything you want, but make sure you put pe.myteria.com for the server address. When you're done, click "Save".

  • Step 4

    Finally, join Myteria and play!

    Scroll down, and click on "Myteria" to join and start playing! Make sure to ask in chat for help if you want to know everything there is to do.
